About the Practice

Meet Scott Wells, FNP-C.

Scott Wells is a board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner licensed in both New Hampshire and Massachusetts. He founded Wells Family Medicine on a simple but powerful conviction: every patient deserves more than a rushed appointment and a prescription.

Scott's approach centers on compassionate, whole-person care with a genuine focus on improving wellness — not just treating illness. By operating as a fee-based, direct-care practice, he eliminates the barriers that insurance-driven medicine puts between a provider and their patients.

Whether visiting you at home, supporting your workforce, or conducting DOT examinations, Scott brings the same level of attentiveness and expertise to every encounter.
